A year as tech lead

December 12, 2021 One-minute read

End of the year has always been a good time for each of us to do a retrospective on how we did for the past year, and this year, I am doing a review on one of the significant changes of my life: being promoted to tech lead. The following is a brief summary of what I have learned and what I am looking forward to in the next year.

Learning

You cannot act like an IC anymore

This hands down is one of the easiest thing I can realize. Much as I am, as a very active contributor before, it was very quick for me to realize my limit. Instead of being able to spend a full day working on a single problem, I now jumps between multiples. It is then I realized I have to spend more time on coaching and mentoring other team members, making them the knowledge expert, which is a more sustainable than having me jump into and handle everything

Look very far in the future, and also plan for the near future
